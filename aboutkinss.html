About KInSS

Welcome to 'UDAY', your trusted partner in delivering innovative and reliable workforce solutions.

Founded on the principles of excellence, integrity, and dedication, we've been at the forefront of the manpower services industry.

Karve Institute of Social Service is a school of social work established on 9th November 1963 in Commemoration of Social reformer Bharat Ratna Maharshi Dhondo Keshav Karve. In a way all the efforts of the Institute are aimed at developing a cadre of committed social workers at various levels through it's formal academic programmes (from Masters Level to the Doctorate level); as well as through, the para-professionals training and capacity building process of staff for Government and Non-Government institutions. The institute is also involved in Professionals research, Consultancy and H.R. The institute is also actively involved in social development initiatives through CSR.

Our Inspiration

Bharatratna Maharshi Dhondo Keshav Karve


Dhondo Keshav Karve (18 April 1858 – 9 November 1962), popularly known as Maharshi Karve, was a social reformer in India in the field of women's welfare He advocated widow remarriage and he himself married a widow. In his honour, Karve nagar in Pune was named after him & Queen's Road in Mumbai (Bombay) was renamed to Maharshi Karve Road. Karve was a pioneer in promoting widows' education. The Government of India awarded him with the highest civilian award, the Bharat Ratna, in 1958, the year of his 100th birthday.

Bhaskar Dhondu Karve

Bhaskarrao Karve inherited the idea of social work, service and human welfare from his father, Bharat Ratna Dhonde Keshav Karve. With this rich legacy of social work and after receiving higher education in Pune and abroad, he established Karve Samaj Seva Sanstha on November 9, 1963. In order to inculcate the true values and principles of social work he founded this institute. This institute imparts professional training in social work with a view to create dedicated manpower to do the social work of community building, and overall development. This institution started with this noble purpose is celebrating its 60th year of its inception.
