Mining and Minerals

The Mining and Minerals sector plays a pivotal role in resource extraction, infrastructure development, and economic growth. Our apprenticeship programs are meticulously designed to provide comprehensive training, preparing apprentices with the skills and expertise needed to excel in the diverse and dynamic world of mining.

Mining Operations and Techniques:Our programs focus on imparting knowledge of mining operations, techniques for mineral extraction, and safety protocols. Apprentices will gain hands-on experience in operating mining equipment and machinery.

Mineral Processing and Refining:Recognizing the importance of refining raw minerals, our programs include specialized training in mineral processing, refining techniques, and adherence to quality standards.

Environmental Sustainability in Mining:Acknowledging the environmental impact, our programs include training in sustainable mining practices, environmental conservation, and the implementation of eco-friendly technologies.

Geology and Exploration:For those interested in exploration, our apprenticeship programs cover geology, mineral exploration techniques, and the use of geological surveys for identifying potential mining sites.

Health and Safety in Mining :Ensuring the well-being of workers is paramount. Our apprenticeship programs emphasize rigorous training on health and safety measures, emergency response procedures, and compliance with mining regulations.

About Naps

National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) is the scheme notified by the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) Government of India on 19th August 2016 providing for financial support to the industry undertaking apprenticeship programmes under the Apprentices Act, 1961.
